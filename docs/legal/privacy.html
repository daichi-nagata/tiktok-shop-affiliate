Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TikTok Shop Affiliate ("the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 TikTok Account Information

When you connect your TikTok account, we access:

  • Your TikTok username and display name
  • Your TikTok profile picture
  • Your TikTok Open ID

2.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the Service:

  • Posts created through the Service
  • Product URLs you add
  • Posting schedules and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Post content to TikTok on your behalf
  • Generate product descriptions using AI
  • Improve the Service

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your data to external servers except:

  • TikTok API (for authentication and posting)
  • Claude API (for AI text generation)
  • imgBB API (for image hosting)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy or as required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API - For authentication and content posting
  • Anthropic Claude API - For AI-generated content
  • imgBB - For image hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs its use of your data.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • API keys and tokens are stored securely
  • Access tokens are encrypted
  • Data is stored locally on your device

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored by the Service
  • Delete your data from the Service
  • Revoke TikTok authorization at any time
  • Request information about data usage

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service. You can delete your data at any time by removing the application.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes through the Service.

12. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
